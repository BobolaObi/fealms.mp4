Timeline Cheat Sheet

Workflow

  1. Import media (Ctrl/Cmd+I or drag to Project).
  2. Drop first clip to create your sequence.
  3. Assemble a rough cut: move, split (S or Ctrl/Cmd+K), ripple trim (Q/W).
  4. Fine tune timing with zoom, snapping, and clip nudges (Alt+←/→).
  5. Add sound layers on audio tracks, balance levels.
  6. Polish: transitions, text, pacing, playback review.
  7. Export or save the project JSON for later.

Timeline Tips

  • Drag the red playhead or use the viewer scrubber for quick preview jumps.
  • Use the zoom slider or the Fit button to frame the whole sequence.
  • Enable snapping to line up edits on the ruler grid.
  • Add extra V/A tracks from the timeline footer when layering footage or sound.
